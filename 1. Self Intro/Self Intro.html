BIO

Fascinated by human motivation and behaviour, I began my career as a psychology researcher. This allowed me to develop practical skills in data analysis, observation, and understanding human behaviour. Now, I hold a Master's in Psychology (Research), and I am driven to bring my research-oriented perspective to the field of UX. My journey has been marked by a genuine curiosity about the human experience and a commitment to applying that understanding to user-centered design. With a focus on bridging the gap between research and design, my goal is to contribute to contribute to functional and empathetic user experiences, grounded in a practical understanding of human behaviour.
